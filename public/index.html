What is Boar?

Boar is an app that lets you share your anger with the world and bond with others who feel the same way. It's a completely anonymous social network where you discover people who hate the same things as you. No emails, no passwords, no social accounts — we collect no data from you.

Our aim is to be the first social network where people discover new things to hate, and other people to hate them with. Love, through hate, so to speak.

Open source

Boar is also open source. We're proud of what we've built and will be sharing it with everyone soon!
